# Code of Conduct

## Our Promise
StudyHub is a welcoming space for all students regardless of
background, experience level, or identity.

## Expected Behavior
- Be kind and respectful in all interactions
- Give constructive feedback, not personal criticism
- Accept feedback graciously — we are all here to learn
- Help others when you can

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory language
- Sharing another student's private information
- Submitting intentionally wrong or harmful study content
- Spamming issues or pull requests

## Enforcement
Violations may result in your contributions being removed
and being blocked from the repository.

## Reporting
If you see a violation, open a private issue labeled `conduct`
or contact the maintainer directly through GitHub.
